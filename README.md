# MediSight

MediSight is a healthcare connectivity platform designed to help patients quickly connect with available doctors and medical services. The system focuses on reducing delays in accessing healthcare by providing real-time information about doctors, clinic queues, and emergency services.

## Overview

Accessing medical care quickly can often be difficult. Patients may not know which doctors are available, clinics can become overcrowded, and emergency response coordination can be slow.

MediSight aims to improve this by creating a digital platform where patients and healthcare providers can interact more efficiently through real-time updates and simple request systems.

## Features

**Doctor–Patient Connectivity**
Patients can browse available doctors and send appointment requests, while doctors can manage and respond to requests through their interface.

**Live Clinic Queue**
Patients can view the current queue at clinics and check estimated waiting times before arriving, helping reduce unnecessary waiting and overcrowding.

**Emergency SOS System**
Patients can send urgent alerts to available doctors in case of emergencies, allowing faster medical attention.

**Ambulance Fleet Tracker**
Users can view nearby ambulances and track their movement in real time, improving emergency response coordination.

## Technology Stack

* **MERN Stack** – MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js
* **Firebase** – Real-time updates, authentication, and notifications

## Future Scope

MediSight can expand into a broader digital healthcare ecosystem with additional capabilities such as:

* AI-based symptom analysis
* Online consultations
* Digital health records
* Medicine reminders
* Integration with hospitals and pharmacies

## Goal

The goal of MediSight is to simplify healthcare access by building a fast, organized platform that connects patients with doctors and emergency services in real time.
